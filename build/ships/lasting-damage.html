Lasting Damage

This ship first appeared in the book Look to Winward—the ship is a General Systems Vehicle (GSV). GSVs are mobile habitats and / or factory ships, they are the largest Culture ship type. They may be home to billions of people.

Note: A GSV built for combat on the eve of the Idiran-Culture War. After it was destroyed in battle, a recorded copy of its mind-state was embodied in a new Mind and incorporated into another GSV of the same class. Its original Mind was later found to have survived the ship’s destruction, and was also incorporated into a new combat GSV.
